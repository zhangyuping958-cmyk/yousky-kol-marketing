# Marketing Department · Org Structure — Design Outline

## Brief
Present the full organizational structure of Yousky's Marketing Department, covering all 4 teams with role breakdowns, headcount, and responsibilities. Dark tech theme matching sellersprite.obuudo.com/claude-code aesthetic.

## Audience
Internal management and cross-functional stakeholders.

## Tone
Professional, clean, tech-forward, information-dense but visually clear.
